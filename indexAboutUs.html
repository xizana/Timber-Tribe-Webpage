About Timber Tribe

Hi there, glad you found us!

We are called Timber Tribe. Our company has been handcrafting wood and resin jewellery in Vancouver, Canadasince December 2015.

Vancouver is a city unlike no other. Wherever you look, you see mountains and water. This is how we becameinspired to create our very first collection – nature rings!

But we also spend a good portion of our lives in the city. The night in Vancouver means downtown is lit by the neon lights of the clubs and bars, shinning on the rain-kissed sidewalks. That’s how we came up with the GLO collection. At night you can be anybody and no-one cares who you really are.

Both Vancouver and Canada have played a key part in the inspiration behind our designs. A blend of etherealfantasy elements grounded on Canadian natural landscapes shapes our creations.

Our collection started with a variety of ring designs and has since expanded to include pendants,nightlights and bracelets. All pieces are made of a combination of different types of wood and jewellery resin as well as additional decorative elements such as blossoms and flakes of gold. Some designs feature silver.

Enjoy exploring our collection to find the perfect piece that matches your unique personality!
